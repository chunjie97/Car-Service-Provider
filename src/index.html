About Us

Carbengkel is a booking platform that enables all car drivers to locate a list of nearest trusted workshop for breakdown, repair and/or service. With Carbengkel, users can request for a quotation or estimated cost of repair first before any repair then book an appointment to either drive to the workshop or arrange for a pickup service.

How It Works

Request Job

Describe the repair work that you need by either taking picture of the problem area or record the sound of the noise problem or describe it in words.

Select a nearby workshop and make an appointment

System will display a list of nearby workshop for you to choose. You can select as many workshop to make an appointment

Appointment confirmed.

A workshop that is available on the date & time of your appointment will confirm the appointment.
